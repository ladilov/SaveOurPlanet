SAVE OUR PLANET

Save Our Planet is not an organization. We are just people who want to live in a beautiful, prosperous nature. We do not force you to do anything, we only ask you to take care of the place where we all live.

MAIN PROBLEMS:










FIRE


Fire is an endless source of light and heat. He kept people warm for a long time and he keeps us warm to this day. But if the fire is not controlled, it can turn into a major problem that will be very difficult to solve.





GARBAGE


Garbage has always been a big problem. But only recently it has grown to incredible proportions. People managed to leave it everywhere - in the ocean, on land and even in space.





DEFORESTATION


Deforestation has been common since ancient times. But nowadays, about 26 million hectares of forest are cut down a year. If this number is not reduced. Soon, our planet will have nothing to breathe.





CARBON DIOXIDE


Many chemicals are released into the atmosphere every day. They poison the air, making it difficult for people to breathe.





HUMANS


It is very unpleasant for us to talk about it. But the main problem is people. Most of the problems with nature are caused by us. We want to pump everything out of the planet without giving it anything in return. We chop down, poison our house trying to cash in on it. But we are not ready for the consequences of our actions.

LET'S SAVE OUR PLANET
